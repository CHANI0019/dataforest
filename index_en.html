Data Forest, The Beginning of a New Opportunity

Data Forest is a decentralized data platform based on Web 3.0 technology. We are creating a new ecosystem where anyone can contribute to data collection and receive fair compensation. Your participation enhances the value of data and becomes a driving force for changing the world.

Learn How to Participate

For Contributors

Anyone with a smartphone can participate in 'Capture-to-Earn'. It offers a new side income opportunity for existing gig economy workers like delivery drivers and ride-sharing drivers.

Innovative Incentives

Base rewards are paid for all valid photo submissions based on a transparent, AI-driven formula (novelty, quality, reputation). You can also earn higher returns through Dynamic Bounties, high-value missions requested by businesses.

Gamification for Engagement

Quests & Challenges, an XP-based Level System, and Leaderboards make data collection more fun and rewarding. Higher levels unlock greater rewards.

User Acquisition Strategy

We attract more participants through App Store Optimization (ASO) with keywords like "make money app" and "gig work", social media ads, and a referral program that offers bonuses for friend referrals.

For Enterprises & Developers

Go beyond simple images. Get 'cryptographically verifiable proof' via API, guaranteeing that a specific visual state existed at a precise time and location.

CPG & Retail Audits

Problem High costs, slow speed, and fragmented information of traditional retail audits.
Solution Obtain verified visual evidence of shelf status, promotions, and inventory levels from thousands of stores nationwide in hours via our On-Demand Retail Intelligence API, preventing sales loss and drastically reducing costs.

Insurance Claim Verification

Problem Poor quality and fraud vulnerability of photos submitted by claimants.
Solution Secure high-quality evidence captured through our secure app with the Verified Incident Report API. Speed up processing and reduce fraud with liveness detection and verified EXIF data.

Flexible Monetization Models

Data consumption is paid for with data credits pegged to the US dollar. We offer various options, including Pay-As-You-Go, Subscription Tiers, and premium Bounty Placement for specific data collection needs.

Technology & Trust

Our tech stack is not just a feature; it's a powerful 'moat' that ensures data reliability and raises the barrier to entry for competitors.

4.1 Trust Layer for Data Integrity

We generate cryptographically verifiable 'proof,' not just images.

Mandatory In-App Camera

To block outdated or manipulated images, all photos must be captured in real-time through the app's secure interface.

Secure EXIF Stamping

High-precision GPS coordinates, timestamps, and device info are recorded and cryptographically hashed with the image file upon capture, ensuring data immutability.

AI-Powered Liveness Detection

To prevent fraudulent activities like re-capturing screens, AI technologies like Amazon Rekognition verify that the camera is capturing a real 3D scene.

4.2 Privacy & Regulatory Compliance

To comply with privacy laws like PIPA (South Korea), identifiable faces and license plates in all uploaded images are automatically blurred through an AI anonymization pipeline. We will initially use commercial APIs and plan to develop our own YOLOv8-based pipeline for long-term cost efficiency.

4.3 Hybrid Quality Assurance (QA)

Stage 1 AI Auto-Check filters low-quality data like blurriness and duplicates. Stage 2 Community Verification allows high-reputation users to act as 'AI Trainers', further enhancing data accuracy and quality through gamified micro-tasks.

Vision & Roadmap

We achieve sustainable growth through a 'Burn-and-Mint' flywheel mechanism, where data demand (token burning by enterprises) is directly linked to data collection incentives (token minting for contributors). As demand grows, more value is returned to the community, driving more data collection.

Phase 1: Foundation & MVP
(Months 1-6)

Build the core technology foundation and validate the initial market. Activities include mobile app (MVP) development, core trust tech integration, and collaboration with pilot clients.

Phase 2: Launch & Initial Scaling
(Months 7-18)

Officially launch the platform and begin acquiring a user base. We will complete the gamification system, intensify marketing efforts, and pursue token exchange listings.

Phase 3: Optimization & Expansion
(Months 19-36)

Enhance cost-efficiency by developing a community-based QA system and our own anonymization pipeline. Expand into new industries like real estate and autonomous driving.
